    UnderDawg eSports & Org

    We are Team Under Dawg an e-sports organization from Bangladesh.
    We are currently competing in E-sports games like
    Valorant, Rainbow six siege, PUBG and CSGO.

    We initially started out as a small clan in 31/7/2021
    and over time we have managed to build a family of talented and hardworking Players.

    We treat our opponents with respect and our fellow clanmates with compassion.

    We refrain from toxic behaviors .

    We know the journey to the top is challenging
    but we are willing to work hard to be one of
    the top E-sports team in South Asia and represent Bangladesh on an
    International Level. We in UD believe that we never lose, We always fight with a hidden power
